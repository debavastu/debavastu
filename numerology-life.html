Numerology & Your Life Path Number

Discover how your Life Path Number shapes your personality, career choices, relationships and overall life direction.

Numerology is the science of numbers that reveals patterns influencing human life. Among all numerology concepts, the Life Path Number is the most important. It represents your core personality, strengths, challenges and purpose.

What is a Life Path Number?

Your Life Path Number is calculated from your date of birth. It reflects the natural direction of your life—how you think, act, learn lessons and move toward success.

How to Calculate Life Path Number

Add all digits of your date of birth until you get a single digit (1–9) or master numbers (11, 22).

Example:
DOB: 11-02-1984 → 1+1+0+2+1+9+8+4 = 26 → 2+6 = 8

Meaning of Life Path Numbers

Life Path 1 – The Leader

Independent, confident and ambitious. Best suited for leadership, entrepreneurship and authority roles.

Life Path 2 – The Diplomat

Peace-loving, emotional and cooperative. Excellent for counseling, HR and relationship-based careers.

Life Path 3 – The Communicator

Creative, expressive and joyful. Good for media, arts, teaching and public interaction roles.

Life Path 4 – The Builder

Disciplined, practical and hardworking. Ideal for engineering, management and structured careers.

Life Path 5 – The Freedom Seeker

Dynamic, adventurous and adaptable. Best in sales, travel, marketing and flexible professions.

Life Path 6 – The Caregiver

Responsible, nurturing and service-oriented. Suitable for healthcare, teaching and healing professions.

Life Path 7 – The Thinker

Analytical, spiritual and introspective. Ideal for research, analysis, astrology and advisory roles.

Life Path 8 – The Achiever

Powerful, goal-oriented and material-focused. Excellent for business, finance and leadership positions.

Life Path 9 – The Humanitarian

Compassionate, wise and service-driven. Best for social work, guidance and global impact roles.
