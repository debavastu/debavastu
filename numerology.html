Numerology

Understanding Your Core Numerology Numbers

Numerology reveals your personality, destiny, emotions, strengths, challenges, and karmic patterns. At DebaVastu, we use a blend of traditional Chaldean name mapping (for name-based numbers) together with DOB calculations for Life Path.

1. Life Path Number

The Life Path is calculated from your Date of Birth (day + month + year). We reduce the sum to a single digit except when a master number (11 or 22) appears.

2. Destiny / Expression Number

Calculated from your full name using the Chaldean mapping (values assigned to letters). This shows your natural talents and long-term achievements.

3. Soul Urge (Heart's Desire)

Sum of the vowels in your name (A E I O U). Shows inner motivations and deepest desires.

4. Personality Number

Sum of consonants in your name. Reflects the outward personality and first impressions.

5. Interpretations

Below the calculator you'll get short meanings for the numbers. Use these as a starting point for deeper readings.
